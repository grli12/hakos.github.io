About Us

The company was founded in 2012 under the name SIDL - Slovak Institute of Transport and Logistics s.r.o. SIDL's main activity was consulting and advisory services. Client requests focused on transport organization, and freight forwarding became the dominant activity, which led to a change in the company's name. Since 2015, we have been providing our services under the name HAKOS Spedition s.r.o.

Our main goal is to provide quality service in securing transport for our customers.

It would not be possible to meet our customers' requirements without our carriers, with whom we strive to build fair and loyal relationships.

Our Services

Our main activity is domestic and international freight forwarding. As part of these services, we can provide you with:

Domestic/International Transport

  • Full truckload
  • Less than truckload
  • Express
  • Temperature-controlled – frozen, refrigerated, temperature-controlled goods
  • Dangerous goods within the meaning of the ADR Agreement
  • Vehicles with hydraulic taillifts, hydraulic arms
  • Oversized and heavy shipments
  • Coil mulda and walking floor trailers
  • Bulk materials

Logistics Services

  • Shipment insurance
  • Short-term/long-term storage up to 500 m²
  • Transshipment, handling of goods / unloading of ISO containers
  • Neutralization of documents

Other Services

  • Professional advice
